Who Are We?

By taking a beautiful journey through space, you will fully experience the exhilaration and wonder of exploring the cosmos.

Is It Safe?

Our space launch system is designed for efficient space travel, while maintaining comfort and leisure for passengers. As with all new technologies, we will take advantage of the inevitable testing and learning opportunities. The design features two solid rocket boosters and a powerful, liquid-fueled, core stage. The capability to land the core stage on a ship in the ocean and fly back to land puts the system in a unique position. The core stage is reusable, which means launching and landing on a particular patch of ocean could allow us to get to any point on the planet in a short amount of time, the most rapid access of any vehicle to anywhere on earth.

Why Should We Go?

Why should we go to space? Space is an exotic frontier, ripe for exploration. The ability to launch objects into space opens new opportunities to explore other planets, moons, asteroids. The vision of exploring every object in the solar system by way of low-cost, reusable spacecraft is exciting and motivating. Additionally, space allows us to explore celestial bodies, such as exoplanets and asteroids, that are too small and distant to be explored from earth.
